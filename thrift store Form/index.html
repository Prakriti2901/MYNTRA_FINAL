Add a New Thrift Product

Guidelines:

  • You can thrift only 1 product every 3 months. Once a product is sold, you can add more.
  • Also an item cannot be thrifted before 2 months of purchase.
  • You will be responsible for paying the delivery charges for product pickup from your home when it is ordered by a customer.
  • The price of the product will be decided by Myntra based on the age of the product.
  • Enter the correct age of the product. Incorrect information can lead to your account being blacklisted.
  • A 10% commission will be charged on each product sold.
